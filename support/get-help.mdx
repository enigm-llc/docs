## Support Boundaries

Enigm support and product guidance do not provide access to message plaintext, secure call content, attachments, media, private cryptographic keys, recovery phrases, or protected user communications.

Security issues should be reported through the designated security communication channel described in [Security Contact](/legal/security-contact).
